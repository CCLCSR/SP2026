⭐ What Makes an Initiative "Innovative"?

Beyond the routine — ideas that spread

Every Swachhata Pakhwada has its cleaning drives and pledge ceremonies. Innovation theme captures what is different this year — initiatives that surprise, solve a real problem in an unexpected way, or are so simple and effective that other areas will immediately want to copy them.

This page collects the best ideas from all 10 CCL areas during SP 2026. After the campaign, the top 3 innovations are submitted to Coal India for national recognition and potential scaling across all subsidiaries. Submit your area's innovative activity via the Google Form by Day 12.

Innovations & Special Initiatives

Tested, documented, and ready to inspire — from CCL areas this fortnight

Digital 🌐

Live Campaign Portal (This Website)

A fully functional GitHub Pages portal documenting the entire Pakhwada in real time — the first of its kind at CCL. Daily news, area data, gallery, and calendar all in one place, accessible to anyone with a phone.

Community 🏛️

Gram Sabha Sanitation Resolution

Kuju area achieved formal Gram Sabha resolutions on open defecation elimination and waste segregation — making cleanliness a matter of village governance, not just a campaign activity.

Economy 💰

Kabad se Kanchan — Scrap Marketplace

SHGs creating a micro-marketplace for waste-based products during the campaign finale — converting the Pakhwada's collected scrap into income-generating products sold at the closing ceremony.

Fitness 🏃

Plogging Championships

Competitive plogging events — runners collect the most litter within a set time — turning cleanliness into a sport. The winner gets a Swachhata Champion medal and their photo on the portal.

Arts 🎭

Waste Sculpture Exhibition

Artists and workers creating sculptures entirely from collected plastic and metal waste — exhibited at the area office for 30 days post-Pakhwada as a permanent reminder of what single-use plastic becomes.

Data 📊

Daily Area Leaderboard

A live scorecard updated daily on this portal tracking each area's participant count, activities completed, and kilos of waste collected — creating friendly inter-area competition and accountability.

Youth 🎤

Swachhata Podcast by Students

Students from CCL schools recording short audio episodes (2–5 min) on their experience of the campaign — broadcast on WhatsApp groups and submitted to All India Radio Ranchi for possible airing.

Infrastructure 🏗️

Bottle Brick Construction Demo

A demonstration structure built using plastic bottles filled with non-recyclable waste as "bottle bricks" — showing that even residual plastic has structural value, with plans to build a garden bench from campaign-collected bottles.

💡 Submit Your Area's Innovation

Each area is encouraged to document one unique initiative from this Pakhwada. The best submission wins the SP 2026 Innovation Award at the closing ceremony and is submitted to Coal India for national recognition.
