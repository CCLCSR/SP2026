IEC Activity Types

Every activity is designed to inform, engage, and inspire lasting behaviour change

🤝

Pledge Ceremonies

Mass pledge drives conducted on Day 1 at all CCL areas simultaneously, setting the tone for 15 days of collective action.

🚶

Swachhata Rallies

Processions through residential colonies and mine sites with banners, slogans, and interactive chants to spread awareness street-by-street.

🚐

Swachhata Rath (Awareness Vehicle)

A decorated awareness vehicle touring each area, broadcasting campaign messages and distributing IEC materials to workers and communities.

📱

Social Media Campaign

Daily posts, selfie booth frames, and WhatsApp broadcasts amplifying field activities — keeping remote stakeholders connected to the campaign.

🎨

Nukkad Natak (Street Plays)

Culture-rooted street theatre performances at community centres and mine gates delivering sanitation messages through storytelling and music.

📋

Door-to-Door Campaigns

Teams visiting households in CCL colonies to explain hygiene practices, distribute leaflets, and collect feedback from residents.

🖼️

Painting & Poster Competitions

Engaging school students and youth in creative expression of cleanliness themes, with winning artwork displayed at area offices.

📣

Public Address Announcements

Regular PA system broadcasts at mine sites and colonies sharing daily updates, tips, and motivational messages during the campaign.

📷

Selfie Booths & Photo Points

Branded selfie booth frames installed at key locations encouraging participants to share campaign moments on social media organically.

What is IEC?

Information, Education and Communication (IEC) is a behaviour-change strategy that delivers targeted messages to specific audiences through appropriate channels. In the context of Swachhata Pakhwada 2026, IEC activities at CCL go beyond posters — they combine mass media, interpersonal communication, and community mobilisation to shift attitudes toward cleanliness, sanitation, and environmental responsibility at every level of the organisation.
