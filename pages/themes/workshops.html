Workshop Programmes

Each session is tailored to its audience — practical, local, and actionable

Hygiene

Personal & Community Hygiene Training

Interactive sessions on handwashing, sanitation practices, and household waste disposal for mine workers and colony residents.

Waste

Solid Waste Segregation Workshop

Hands-on training on wet/dry/hazardous waste segregation using colour-coded bins, with demos at mine canteens and colony common areas.

SHG

SHG Capacity Building — Kabad se Kanchan

Self-Help Groups trained in waste-to-resource techniques — converting scrap and discarded materials into products of value.

Health

Menstrual Hygiene Management

Sensitisation workshops for women workers and community members, covering hygiene practices and disposal of sanitary materials.

Water

Water Conservation Awareness

Technical briefings on rainwater harvesting, water body protection, and reducing water wastage in mining operations and colonies.

School

Eco-Club & Youth Leader Training

Nurturing student leaders in schools near CCL operations who can independently run cleanliness drives and environmental activities.

🎯 Who Attends

  • Mine workers and supervisors
  • Self-Help Group (SHG) members
  • Colony resident welfare associations
  • School teachers and Eco-Club students
  • Sanitation and housekeeping staff
  • Panchayat and village community leaders
  • Health and safety officers
  • Women workers and Mahila Mandals
